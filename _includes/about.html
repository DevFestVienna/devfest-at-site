About {{site.eventName}}

DevFest Vienna is a community event for hobby & professional developers and students, with topics around Mobile, Social Media, Cloud & Web. Upcoming technologies which will be relevant 2016 and beyond are the focus of this conference.

Last year we had more than 250 attendees and we expect around 300 participants for 2015.

Conference

GDG Vienna, JSUG and AndroidHeads Vienna bring to you this amazing conference. Awesome speakers and lots of fun!

Socializing

Join the afterparty with free drinks & entertainment! Relax after the sessions, win some prizes at the raffle, and socialize with peers from the developer community.

OpenHack

Full-day free-style OpenHack! Want some real teambuilding on Sunday? Grab your team and join our OpenHack!
